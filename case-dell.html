Dell Technologies — HPC & Data Protection

Where high‑performance computing meets predictive cyber‑resilience.

Overview

At Dell Technologies, I led global product marketing for two mission‑critical portfolios: High‑Performance Computing (HPC) and Data Protection & Cyber Recovery. These platforms powered hospitals, research institutions, universities, and enterprises running large‑scale simulations, imaging workloads, and mission‑critical data operations.

My work connected advanced compute infrastructure with AI‑driven cyber‑resilience — positioning Dell as a leader in both performance and protection for the world’s most demanding environments.

Problem

Customers across healthcare, research, and enterprise IT were facing two accelerating challenges:

  • HPC workloads were exploding — imaging, genomics, simulation, and AI models were outpacing infrastructure budgets.
  • Ransomware was evolving faster than human detection — corrupting backups, encrypting data, and bypassing traditional defenses.
Organizations needed infrastructure that was not only fast — but intelligent, resilient, and predictive.

Solution

I repositioned Dell’s HPC and Data Protection portfolio as an integrated AI‑driven performance and cyber‑resilience engine:

  • HPC clusters optimized for imaging, genomics, simulation, and research workloads
  • Predictive anomaly detection to identify ransomware and corruption patterns in real time
  • Automated cyber‑recovery workflows to restore clean data quickly and safely
  • Unified dashboards for operators, researchers, and IT teams
  • AI‑powered onboarding that scaled to 5,000+ users per year
This narrative connected performance, protection, and intelligence into a single, compelling value story.

Impact

The combined HPC + Cyber Recovery GTM strategy delivered measurable results:

  • 42% improvement in competitive win rates
  • 37% market share growth in cyber‑resilience
  • 30% increase in new‑user adoption through AI‑powered onboarding
  • 25% reduction in time‑to‑value for new HPC and data protection deployments
  • Accelerated research timelines for hospitals and universities
  • Reduced ransomware recovery times from days to hours
This work became a cornerstone of my Physical AI narrative — demonstrating how intelligence can protect and accelerate the world’s most critical data and compute systems.
